Project 1

Spacial forms/ Utylity form/ 2016/17

The design was made on the basis of inspiration with the urban landscape. It differs from hangers with this motif in that usually the city is presented in a flat form. We decided to design a hanger model resembling buildings in 3D form. This makes the hanger look like a simplified model of skyscrapers placed on a glass platform. This platform is designed to fulfill the role of a shelf for shoes, which is an additional advantage of the design. Thanks to this, our hanger performs two functions at the same time. For visualization, we decided to use the 3D INVENTOR computer program.

Project 2

Product design/ Ergonomics in design/ 2017/18

The main assumption of the project was to get rid of the surface creating a door handle to leave the edges alone. In addition, these edges were to build an inspired door handle geometric products. The design was intended to be printed in a 3D printer. For visualization I decided to use a computer program INVENTOR Professional. The project is presented on renderings in steel color. Creation of an openwork door handle, without large surfaces makes her form seem lighter.

Project 3

Interior design/ Commercial interior/ 2018/19

The project was made on the basis of research inspiration regarding the psychology of food and the shape of the main course pizza divided into triangles. Bright colors were used stimulating the desire to eat, then they were complemented by bright, soft colors and wood so that the viewer does not strain the eye. In addition, the shape of the chairs and sofa reminds pizza without one piece. We decided to use it for visualization SketchUp computer program.
